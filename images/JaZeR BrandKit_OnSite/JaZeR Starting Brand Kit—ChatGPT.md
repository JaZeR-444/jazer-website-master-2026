Typography

Logo usage rules

Clear space equal to the J height. Minimum width 160 px digital. Use light or dark versions for contrast. On busy art add a thin outline or glow. Do not skew, recolor outside the palette, or apply heavy drop shadows.



Imagery and motifs

Night city reflections, long exposure light trails, cosmic scenes, and techno mysticism. Keep the center focus with a radial glow or vignette.

Things to consider next



Font handling in Canva. The SVG keeps live text. If Canva substitutes fonts, upload Orbitron and Montserrat or outline the text before final export.



Contrast checks. Ensure body text meets WCAG contrast on Night Black. Use Stardust White or Soft Slate for long copy.



Glow restraint. Keep glow at 2 to 4 px blur for legibility. Increase only for large hero art.



Single color marks. Prepare white and graphite versions for emboss, laser, or merch printing.



Print workflows. CMYK values in the kit are calculated. Ask your print shop for their profile and soft proof before large runs.



Versioning. Add a version number in the brand kit. Log changes to colors, logos, and templates.
